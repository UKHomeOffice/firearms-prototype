Requirements Section 5 authority application requirements

You'll need to provide the following information to submit an application. You should have this information to hand, as you'll need to complete the process in a single sitting.

1. Company or sole traderâ€™s name

If your application is successful, the S5 authority will be granted under the name of the company or sole trader.

2. How the weapons, component parts or ammunition will be obtained

Options will be provided with guidance to help you explain how you'll obtain them.

3. What the weapons, component parts or ammunition will be used for

Options will be provided with guidance to help you explain how you'll use them.

4. Documental evidence showing a business need

It's essential that there's a proven business need for an S5 authority to be granted. You must provide evidence that backs up the reasons you've given in Steps 2 and 3.

5. Any address(es) where the weapons, component parts or ammunition will be stored, if necessary

If there's storage for the weapons, component parts and/or ammunition, you'll need to list each storage address.

6. Section types and quantities of weapons, component parts and ammunition

Your authority will cover only those weapons, component parts and ammunition types and quantities you've listed.

7. Authority holder(s)

You can have up to two responsible people named on the authority. You'll need to provide the home address, place and date of birth, and nationality for each.

8. Correspondence details

Who we can contact regarding this application. You'll need to provide name, address, phone number and email address.


By submitting an application, you agree to the Home Office sharing information with the police and HMRC so they can carry out their necessary checks.
