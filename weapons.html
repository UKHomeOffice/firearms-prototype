Which categories do your weapons fall under?

Select all that apply



Help with section 5(1)(a)

This includes all weapons which are so designed or adapted that two or more missiles can be successively discharged without repeated pressure on the trigger.



Help with section 5(1)(ab)

This includes any self-loading or pump-action rifled gun other than one which is chambered for a .22 rimfire cartridges.



Help with section 5(1)(aba)

This includes any firearm which either has a barrel less than 30cm in length or is less than 60cm in length overall, other than an air weapon, a muzzle-loading gun or a firearm designed as a signalling apparatus.



Help with section 5(1)(ac)

This includes any self-loading or pump-action smooth-bore gun which is not an air weapon or chambered for .22 rimfire cartridges and either has a barrel less than 24 inches in length or is less than 40 inches in length overall.



Help with section 5(1)(ad)

This includes any smooth-bore revolver gun other than one which is chambered for 9mm rimfire cartridges or a muzzle-loading gun.



Help with section 5(1)(ae)

This includes any rocket launcher, or any mortar, for projecting a stabilised missile, other than a launcher or mortar designed for line-throwing or pyrotechnic purposes or as a signalling apparatus.



Help with section 5(1)(af)

This includes any air rifle, air gun or air pistol which uses, or is designed or adapted for use with a self-contained gas cartridge system.



Help with section 5(1)(b)

This includes any weapon of whatever description designed or adapted for the discharge of any noxious liquid, gas or other thing.



Help with section 5(1A)(a)

This includes any firearm which is disguised as another object.



Help with section 5(1A)(b)

This includes any rocket or ammunition not falling within section 5(1)(c) which consists in or incorporates a missile designed to explode on or immediately before impact and is for military use.



Help with section 5(1A)(c)

This includes any launcher or other projecting apparatus that's not included in section 5(1)(ae) and is designed to be used with a 5(1A)(b) rocket or ammunition, or with section 5(1)(c) ammunition.
