Which categories do your ammunition fall under?

Select all that apply



Help with section 5(1)(c)

This includes any cartridge with a bullet designed to explode on or immediately before impact, any ammunition containing or designed or adapted to contain any such noxious thing as is mentioned in section 5(1)(b) and, if capable of being used with a firearm of any description, any grenade, bomb (or other missile), or rocket or shell designed to explode as aforesaid.



Help with section 5(1A)(d)

This includes any ammunition for military use which consists in or incorporates a missile designed so that a substance contained in the missile will ignite on or immediately before impact.



Help with section 5(1A)(e)

This includes any ammunition for military use which consists in or incorporates a missile designed, on account of its having a jacket and hard-core to penetrate armour plating, armour screening or body armour.



Help with section 5(1A)(f)

This includes any ammunition which incorporates a missile designed or adapted to expand on impact.



Help with section 5(1A)(g)

This includes anything designed to be projected as a missile from any weapon and is designed to be, or has been, incorporated in section 5(1A) ammunition, or any ammunition which would fall within section 5(1A) if it weren't specified in any part of section 5(1).
